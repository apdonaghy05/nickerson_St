OUR MISSION

At Nickerson St. Painting L.L.C it is our mission to provide outstanding service throughout the Seattle area. We strive to provide our valued customers with superior results through meticulous attention to detail, reliability, and use of cutting edge methods.

Quality is our first priority, whether we're painting just one room, or the whole exterior of the house. Every step of the way we aim to go one step further than the competition. With experience and the desire to constantly improve our craft we will give you the finest paint job around at competitive prices.

We work closely with you during the process so that you get what you want in the finished product. We will give you our suggestions based on our experience, but the decisions of how you want to transform your house are completely up to you.

MEET THE OWNER

Ryan Donaghy holds a BS degree in Geology from Western Washington University and also posesses twelve-plus years of professional painting experience. He has painted hundreds of houses and condo buildings and is exceedingly knowledgeable about anything to do with the craft.

Ryan was born in Seattle, Washington and he now resides in Magnolia with his beautiful wife and his dog, Ollie. He is very interested in anything to do with geology and is an avid sports fan. He loves the Mariners, Huskies and is a season-ticket holder to the Seahawks.
